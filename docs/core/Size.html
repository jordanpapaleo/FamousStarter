Size

Size [constructor] 

The size primitive is being used internally by the RenderContext to manage and update its respective transform matrix. It doesn't expose user-facing APIs, but instead is being exposed on the RenderContext level in form of various methods, e.g. setProportional and setAbsolute.

new Size(context)
