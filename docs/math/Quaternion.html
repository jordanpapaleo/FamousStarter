Quaternion

Quaternion [constructor] 

A vector-like object used to represent rotations. If theta is the angle of rotation, and (x', y', z') is a normalized vector representing the axis of rotation, then w = cos(theta/2), x = -sin(theta/2)x', y = -sin(theta/2)y', and z = -sin(theta/2)*z'.
